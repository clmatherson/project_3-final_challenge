Analysis

Brief Description

We knew going into this project it would be very challenging because object detection and machine learning on pictures was completely new to the entire group. During the two weeks there were many challenges that needed to be overcome, and most were.

Model Analysis

Having good images for our model is critical for training with the need for different views and oriatation for training. Because we lacked good variety of images the for our model we had mixed results with our loss function as well as our accuracy during object-detection demonstration.
There are multiple models to choose from the Tensorflow Github page. Model ssd_mobilenet_v2_pfnlite_320x320_coco17_tpu-8 was chosen

Results

Training takes 6 to 20 hours to train a model depending on computer hardware

Improvements

While our intentions were pure, the results have room for improvement and two weeks was not nearly enough time to work on all the moving parts.
