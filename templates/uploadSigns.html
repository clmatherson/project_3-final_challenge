Train your own signs
On this page you can train the machine learning model to recognize up to 10 signs. They can be the correct format or an abbreviated format. This can be done by pressing the button with the sign below the video image while performing the sign in front of your camera feed. The more repetitions of each sign you want recognized the better the model will be trained and the higher the degree of accuracy in sign recognition. After recording the signs press the train button, this will train the machine learning model; after it is finished training you should begin to see the labels of the signs you trained below video as you sign them.
The eventual goal of this page is to make it where users can changed the words on the buttons to any word they wish to input. We would also like to change the training input mechanism, as pressing down on the button and signing can be difficult as a lot of words require two hands. You may also notice a save button, it does work, it will save the trained model in a json and binary file to your download folder. The next step is allowing users to upload their saved trained model onto an existing platform that will allow them to not worry about constantly having to retrain the model for the words they wish to use while out at a store or restraunt.
